# Privacy Policy  
**Insights for App Store Connect**

**Effective Date:** February 10, 2026  
**Last Updated:** February 10, 2026

Insights for App Store Connect (“**Insights**,” “**we**,” “**us**,” or “**our**”) provides a mobile and desktop client application for viewing App Store Connect analytics, transactions, customer reviews, and managing review responses on Apple platforms (iOS and macOS).

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when you use Insights.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account & Authentication Information
When you sign in using **Sign in with Apple**, we process:

- Apple Sign-In identity token
- Apple subject identifier
- Email address (if provided by Apple)
- Optional user role

Authentication tokens processed by our backend include:

- Access token (15-minute lifetime)
- Refresh token (7-day lifetime)

---

### 1.2 App Store Connect Data
When you connect your App Store Connect account, we process business data retrieved from Apple’s App Store Connect API, including:

- App metadata (app ID, SKU, title, bundle ID, locale, categories, privacy policy URL)
- Analytics aggregates (downloads, sessions, purchases, crashes, device, platform, territory, source)
- Transaction and sales records (SKU, proceeds, currency, territory, subscription events)
- Customer reviews (title, body, rating, reviewer nickname, territory, date)
- Developer review responses (body and state)

This data is used solely to provide Insights’ functionality.

---

### 1.3 Notifications
If you enable notifications:

- Notification permission status
- Apple Push Notification service (APNs) device token

Push notifications are optional and can be disabled at any time in system settings.

---

### 1.4 Operational & Log Data
Our backend collects limited operational data for security and reliability purposes:

- Request metadata (HTTP method, path, status, latency)
- Masked user identifiers
- Partially redacted IP addresses
- Metrics and counters

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Authenticate users and manage sessions
- Retrieve and display App Store Connect data
- Enable customer review management and responses
- Provide analytics and reporting features
- Deliver optional push notifications
- Maintain security, prevent abuse, and monitor system health
- Improve performance and reliability

---

## 3. Local Storage on Your Device

Insights stores certain information locally:

### Keychain (Secure Storage)
- App Store Connect credentials
- Authentication tokens
- User identifiers and roles

Keychain items may be synchronized using iCloud Keychain, depending on system settings.

### UserDefaults
- Selected app ID
- Analytics filters and timeframes
- Onboarding and demo state
- Navigation state

### Local Files
- Cached app summaries and analytics data stored under the app’s Application Support directory

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We share data only with the following parties when necessary:

- **Apple**
  - Sign in with Apple
  - App Store Connect API
  - Apple Push Notification service (if enabled)
- **Infrastructure Providers**
  - Cloud hosting, logging, and monitoring providers used to operate the service

We do **not** sell personal data or share it for advertising purposes.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- Authentication data is retained while your account is active.
- You may disconnect App Store Connect at any time, which deletes stored credentials and cached data.
- Logs and operational data are retained for a limited period for security and diagnostics.

Retention durations may evolve as the product matures.

---

## 6. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the app.

When you delete your account:

- Your authentication records and sessions are deleted from our backend
- Local credentials, tokens, and cached data are cleared

Historical analytics and App Store Connect data may be retained in aggregated or anonymized form.

---

- Backup and restore procedures
