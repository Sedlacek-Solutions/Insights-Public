# Insights for App Store Connect

Take control of your App Store business with **Insights for App Store Connect** — a fast, secure client for viewing analytics, transactions, and customer reviews, and responding to feedback, all in one place.

Built for indie developers, teams, and agencies who want visibility without friction, Insights connects directly to Apple’s App Store Connect APIs and presents your data in a clean, focused interface on iOS and macOS.

---

## Key Features

### App Store Analytics
- View downloads, sessions, purchases, and crashes
- Break down performance by device, platform, source, and territory
- Quickly switch between apps and time ranges

### Sales & Transactions
- Inspect sales and proceeds by SKU, currency, and region
- Track subscription events and periods
- Understand trends without exporting spreadsheets

### Customer Reviews
- Read ratings and reviews across territories
- Respond directly to customer reviews
- Monitor review activity in one centralized view

### Secure App Store Connect Access
- Sign in with Apple
- Use your own App Store Connect API keys
- Credentials stored securely in the system Keychain
- Revoke access at any time

### Native Apple Platform Experience
- Designed for iOS and macOS
- Fast, responsive, and keyboard-friendly
- No web wrappers or embedded dashboards

---

## Privacy & Security

- Your App Store Connect data is used only to provide app functionality
- No advertising or data selling
- Secure Keychain storage for credentials and tokens
- Optional push notifications

---

## Who It’s For

- Indie developers managing one or more apps
- Small teams and agencies
- Anyone who wants a focused App Store Connect client without the clutter
